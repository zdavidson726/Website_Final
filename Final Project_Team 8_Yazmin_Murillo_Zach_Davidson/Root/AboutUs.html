Background

Cruz Landscaping, Inc. is a full service, year-round landscaping company that has been meeting the needs of residential and commercial clients in and around the greater St.Louis area for over two decade. The company’s reputation is built on its commitment to the timely execution of quality work. From custom design to comprehensive installation, our experienced and knowledgeable staff will create a landscape that provides a more functional, pleasing and personal environment. We also offer complete full-season maintenance services to keep our client’s landscape investment looking its best. Our certified horticulturists will create radiant color and our turf professionals will keep our customer’s lawns green and healthy year-round. A dedicated group of seasoned managers assures competent oversight of projects. Members of our staff hold 2-4 year degrees in agricultural studies and are active in continuing education programs and certifications within the field. We hope that you will consider Cruz Landscaping for your residential or commercial property needs. We look forward to helping you achieve your vision. Together we can enhance the beauty and value of your property. Contact us for a no obligation estimate… beautiful properties begin here!
